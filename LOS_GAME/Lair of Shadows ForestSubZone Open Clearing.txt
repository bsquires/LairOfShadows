Open Clearing

The forest is very dense, with very few areas
where the sun peaks through its branches.
The few places where such places exist may
seem like a beacon of safety in the near 
constant darkness of the forest, but they
are merely a trap used to lure in
unsuspecting prey. The predators of the
forest have learned to wait near these
places, especially since water tends to
gather there. Whenever you see water in the
forest, stay on the lookout...
